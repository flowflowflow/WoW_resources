Basic Info Tables - gearing with greens in TBC
I haven't seen a consolidated list like this that I could show to some newer players I'm playing with, so I've compiled some stuff here. There may be some errors because I'm a huntard and copy/paste things incorrectly sometimes.
If you spot something wrong, just offer up a correction please
What's this for?
New or returning players with gear worse than BWL will likely find upgrades on the AH for near vendor price by searching for the appropriate suffixes based on their spec. For example, a communal geared, boost paladin may be able to grab a set of "of the sorcerer" armor to get some int, stam, and SP before entering dungeons to tank, or some "of the physician" gear to heal more effectively.
At present, armor is selling for <1g more than its vendor price on the AH, so you can buy a piece of armor for 5g, use it until you get an upgrade, and then vendor it for 4g. It's a very efficient way to equip yourself and replace gear from boosting or leveling through Azeroth.
